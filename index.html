About Us

Experience and Expertise

Established in 2004 in Kharagpur, West Bengal, was locally known as Sikandar Sound System, Urban Spark has been at the forefront of providing top-tier sound systems for professional events. With two decades of experience, we have honed our expertise in delivering exceptional audio experiences for a diverse range of events.

Comprehensive Sound Solutions

Urban Spark specializes in supplying state-of-the-art sound systems tailored for various professional shows, including live concerts, corporate events, and music festivals. Our commitment to quality ensures that every event is a sonic success, enhancing the overall experience for attendees.

Client-Centric Approach

At Urban Spark, we prioritize our clients' needs, offering customized sound solutions to meet the unique demands of each event. Our dedicated team of professionals works closely with event organizers to ensure seamless integration and flawless audio performance, making us the trusted choice for sound systems in the industry.
